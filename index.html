Privacy Policy - Warranty Maintenance

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Warranty Maintenance, an application designed to help users store appliance warranty details, schedule maintenance, and manage service history. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How we use and protect your data
  • Your rights regarding your information
  • How you can contact us

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use the app, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User role (User / Technician / Supervisor)

1.2 Appliance & Warranty Data

  • Appliance name and category (AC, TV, Fan, etc.)
  • Purchase date and warranty period
  • Warranty documents uploaded by the user
  • Service and maintenance history

1.3 Usage Data

  • App activity logs
  • Device information
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Manage and display warranty details
  • Send warranty expiry and maintenance reminders
  • Track service requests and technician assignments
  • Maintain service history records
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Secure authentication and access control
  • Encrypted data storage and transmission
  • Restricted access based on user roles
  • Regular system updates

4. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and update your personal information
  • Upload or remove warranty documents
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data

5. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time. Upon deletion, all personal data, warranty records, and service history associated with your account will be permanently removed from our system.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Cloud storage providers for secure data storage
  • Notification services for reminders and alerts

These services comply with standard data protection practices.

7. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app and on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us:

  • Email: support@yourappdomain.com
  • App: Use the “Contact Us” option inside the application
